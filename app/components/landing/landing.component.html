Gainesville is Open For Business!

The Department of Doing is standing by to help you research, open, and grow your business in Gainesville, Fla.

Getting Started with the Right Tools and Support

The Department of Doing acts as a business coach, identifying resources and expertise needed about the local market, available locations and spaces, and how to navigate the upcoming set-up approval processes.

Navigating Setup and Processes

The Department of Doing acts as a navigator, guiding customers through the City processes including a new tailored First Step meeting, to help understand relevant planning, zoning, and permitting constraints.

Playing Many Roles and Balancing all the Pieces

The Department of Doing acts as a connector, enabling new business owners to identify and source necessary vendors, partners, and services needed to open. It checks in on a yearly basis to connect to local growth opportunities and ongoing support
